# Ethical Use and Defensive Purpose

## Intended Use

This **Phishing URL Detection System** is designed and must be used **only for**:

- **Defensive security**: Identifying potentially phishing URLs in emails, logs, or user-reported links within your organization.
- **SOC and Blue Team operations**: Triage, investigation, and training.
- **Research and education**: Understanding ML-based threat detection and feature design for cybersecurity.
- **Authorized testing**: Evaluating the tool on your own systems or with explicit permission.

## Prohibited Use

You must **not** use this system to:

- Create, host, or distribute phishing sites or campaigns.
- Target individuals or organizations without authorization.
- Bypass or replace proper security controls or legal/compliance requirements.
- Conduct offensive operations outside explicitly authorized scope.
- Automate mass scanning of third-party systems without permission.

## Responsibility

- **Accuracy**: The model can produce false positives and false negatives. Use it as an **aid to human judgment**, not as the sole decision for blocking or accusing.
- **Data**: Train only on datasets you are allowed to use; respect privacy and terms of use of any third-party data sources.
- **Compliance**: Ensure use aligns with your organizationâ€™s policies, GDPR/privacy rules, and local laws.

## Summary

Use this tool **defensively and responsibly**. When in doubt, restrict to internal, authorized, and educational contexts and always combine with human review and proper security processes.
